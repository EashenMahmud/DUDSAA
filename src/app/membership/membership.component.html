Membership Types:

General Member: Graduated from any UG/PG program of DUDSAA paying annual subscription fees.

Life-member: Graduate from any UG or PG program of DUDSAA paying one time life membership fee.

Honorary member: Executive committee may offer honorary membership to local/ international non-alumni renowned personnel who will enhance the prestige and fame of the DUDSAA Alums in the form of service, donation, sponsorships etc. Honorary members will not have any voting power or take part in the election.

Membership Fees:

Registration/ Signup Fee (one time): Tk. 300.00 (Waived for life membership)

General Member Annual Fee: Tk. 1,200.00

Life Member fees: Tk. 25,000.00
